Event Agenda

Time: 12:00 PM – 5:00 PM (5 hours)

  1. 12:00 PM – 12:15 PM Introduction and Event Kickoff (15 minutes)

    • Welcome participants.
    • Introduce the event's objectives, agenda, and judges.
    • Provide an overview of the judging criteria and expectations.

  2. 12:15 PM – 12:45 PM Panel Discussion with Judges (30 minutes)

    • Judges share experiences transitioning from student to young professional.
    • Q&A Session: Participants ask questions about careers, challenges, and growth.

  3. 12:45 PM – 1:15 PM Ideation Session Part 1 (30 minutes)

    • Teams brainstorm ideas.
    • Begin developing UI wireframes and system diagrams.
    • Mentors and judges provide initial feedback.

  4. 1:15 PM – 1:45 PM Pizza Break (30 minutes)

    • Relax and enjoy pizza and soda.
    • Casual networking opportunity.

  5. 1:45 PM – 3:15 PM Ideation Session Part 2 (1 hour 30 minutes)

    • Continue refining ideas.
    • Finalize wireframes and system diagrams for the pitch.
    • Ongoing support from mentors and judges.

  6. 3:15 PM – 4:15 PM Pitching Session (1 hour)

    • Each team presents their ideas (approximately 5-6 minutes per team).
    • Presentations to judges and fellow participants.

  7. 4:15 PM – 4:35 PM Judges' Deliberation (20 minutes)

    • Judges score and discuss each team's presentation.
    • Participants can network or relax during this time.

  8. 4:35 PM – 5:00 PM Closing Ceremony and Prize Distribution (25 minutes)

    • Announce winners.
    • Distribute prizes.
    • Thank you notes and closing remarks.
